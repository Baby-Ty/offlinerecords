Emergency Records Help

Quick Start

  1. Choose the type of record you need to access
  2. Select your facility and role from the cards
  3. Records will open automatically for viewing or printing

Record Types

  • Face Sheets: Patient demographics, contact info, diagnoses
  • MED (MARS): Medication administration records
  • TRE (TARS): Treatment administration records
  • OTH (Orders): Medical orders and prescriptions

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Esc - Close dialogs or go back
  • Enter - Select highlighted item
